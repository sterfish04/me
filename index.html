The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is a psychological tool used to measure individual personality types.

It assesses people based on four dimensions:
1. Extraversion (E) vs. Introversion (I)
2. Sensing (S) vs. Intuition (N)
3. Thinking (T) vs. Feeling (F)
4. Judging (J) vs. Perceiving (P)
